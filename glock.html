The Modular Optic System (MOS)

What is MOS?

The Glock Modular Optic System simplifies mounting of popular optic sights without costly custom machining of the pistol’s slide.

The Glock MOS allows the shooter to mount the sight of their choice, easily and quickly using only a few tools.

OPTICS SHOWN ON GUNS ARE NOT INCLUDED WITH MOS PURCHASE.
